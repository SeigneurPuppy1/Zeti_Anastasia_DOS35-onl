Yesterday it was the twenty-sixth of October. It was Harry’s birthday. Early in the morning Mummy and Daddy came into his room and said, “Many happy returns of the day! There are three little presents for you in the living-room.”
So Harry got up quickly and ran to the living-room. He opened the door of the living-room and he saw the presents on the table. Mummy gave him a book. Daddy gave him a new toy-car. His sister gave him a box of sweets.
